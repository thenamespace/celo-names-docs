title: "Transfer Guide"
description: "How to transfer a Celo Name safely"
---

Overview of transferring ownership and recommended steps:

1. Transfer name ownership to the recipient.
2. Recipient sets their resolver and records.
3. Recipient sets primary name (reverse record).

<Note>
See Base reference: [Basename Transfer Guide](https://docs.base.org/base-account/basenames/basename-transfer).
</Note>
